# SCREENSHOT LIST — YoutubeFocus

Capture these 5 screenshots for promo use. Use Chrome at 1440×900 unless noted. Dark popup theme preferred.

---

## Screenshot 1

**Filename:** `01-youtube-before-focus.png`
**URL / State:** YouTube homepage (`youtube.com`) with extension OFF — default YouTube state
**What to highlight:** The Shorts shelf at the top, the packed home feed of thumbnails, the sidebar with trending/recommended content
**Why it matters:** The "before" state. You can't sell a solution without showing the problem. This screenshot makes people feel the overwhelm they already recognize.

---

## Screenshot 2

**Filename:** `02-youtube-after-focus.png`
**URL / State:** YouTube homepage with Focus Mode ON — distraction-free state
**What to highlight:** The clean page — no Shorts shelf, no home feed, no sidebar noise. Show the emptiness as a positive
**Why it matters:** The "after" state. The contrast with Screenshot 1 is the entire pitch. Pair them side-by-side in any promo asset.

---

## Screenshot 3

**Filename:** `03-popup-dark-mode.png`
**URL / State:** Extension popup open (click icon in Chrome toolbar) — dark theme, Focus Mode toggle ON, Timed Lock buttons visible
**What to highlight:** The clean dark popup UI, the enabled toggle state, the 25/50/90/custom time buttons
**Why it matters:** Introduces the UI before someone installs. Looks professional, not like a hobbyist hack. Small popup UI is hard to judge without seeing it — this removes the uncertainty.

---

## Screenshot 4

**Filename:** `04-timed-lock-active.png`
**URL / State:** Popup open with a Timed Lock session actively running — countdown timer showing remaining time
**What to highlight:** The countdown timer display in the popup, the locked state indicator
**Why it matters:** Timed Lock is the feature that differentiates this from every basic content blocker. Show it running, not just as a button. Active timers are compelling.

---

## Screenshot 5

**Filename:** `05-video-no-distractions.png`
**URL / State:** A YouTube video page with Focus Mode ON — player visible, comments and related videos hidden
**What to highlight:** The clean video player with nothing below (no comments) and nothing on the right (no related videos)
**Why it matters:** This is the core promise delivered. You open a video, you watch the video, the environment doesn't pull you away. Show this and the value proposition sells itself.
