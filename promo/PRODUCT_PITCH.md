# PRODUCT PITCH — YoutubeFocus

## One-Sentence Pitch

YoutubeFocus is a Chrome extension that surgically removes every distraction from YouTube — Shorts, feeds, comments, recommendations, and notification noise — so you can watch what you came for and actually stop.

---

## Product Description

YouTube is engineered to keep you watching. YoutubeFocus is engineered to let you leave. It's a Chrome extension (Manifest V3) that hides the parts of YouTube designed to hijack your attention: the Shorts shelf, the home feed, related video recommendations, comments, Trending, and Explore. What's left is exactly what you opened the tab for — the video.

Focus Mode is a one-click toggle that hides the noise instantly. Timed Lock goes further: set a 25, 50, 90, or custom-minute session window, and distractions stay hidden until your timer runs out. The extension runs entirely on your device — no remote code, no analytics, no data collection, no account. It stores only your preferences locally via Chrome's storage API. It's ready for the Chrome Web Store.

---

## Best 5 Features

- **Focus Mode toggle** — one click to hide all distractions; one click to bring them back
- **Timed Lock sessions** — 25 / 50 / 90 / custom minutes; distractions locked away until the session ends
- **Hides the right things** — Shorts shelf, home feed, related videos, comments, Trending, Explore, notification bells
- **Light and dark popup themes** — the extension UI matches your preference without touching YouTube's own settings
- **Zero data collection** — no analytics, no remote code, no account; local storage only

---

## 30-Second Demo Flow

1. Open YouTube — show the normal state: Shorts, home feed, sidebar noise, comments all visible
2. Click the YoutubeFocus extension icon in the Chrome toolbar — the popup opens, clean and minimal
3. Toggle Focus Mode ON — the page updates instantly; Shorts shelf gone, home feed gone, sidebar cleared
4. Click "Timed Lock" and select 25 minutes — the session starts; button changes to show remaining time
5. Open a video — only the video and player controls are visible; no comments, no related videos tempting you
6. Click the extension icon again to show the active session timer counting down

## Target Audience

- Students and knowledge workers using YouTube for tutorials, lectures, or research
- Anyone who opens YouTube with a specific video in mind and ends up watching for two hours
- People trying to use YouTube during a Pomodoro or deep work session
- Privacy-focused users who don't trust browser extensions that phone home
- Developers and power users who want a lightweight, no-bloat Chrome extension
